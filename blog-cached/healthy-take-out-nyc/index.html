The 10 Best Healthy Take Out Options in NYC

Looking for the best healthiest delivery ideas in your area? Bask is the easy new way to order healthy, delicious food to your doorstep. After learning about your health goals, taste preferences and any special desires or needs, Bask’s intelligent food filtering algorithm gets to work figuring out which menu items are the best match for what you’re craving, day or night.

Broccoli and Tofu with Black Bean Sauce Lunch

Lightly sauteed with fresh mushrooms, onions, carrots and peppers. Served over brown rice.
Where to get it:
Village Natural – Asian, Sandwiches, Healthy, Salads, Vegetarian, Dinner

The Green Kitchen Sesame Wrap

Grilled chicken, avocado, cucumber, tomato, spinach and sesame ginger dressing. Served on a choice of wrap with mesclun greens and house dressing.
Where to get it: Green Kitchen – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Grill, Breakfast, Wraps, Hamburgers, Dinner

Grilled Salmon Salad

Grilled salmon, carrots, onion, tomatoes, cucumbers and jicama.
Choose a dressing — Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
Where to get it: Brooklyn Natural Cafe – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Wraps, Smoothies and Juices, Dinner

Fish Tandoori

Today’s fresh fish marinated in herbs and spices, barbecued over charcoal and served with mushrooms and spinach on the side. Your choice of red snapper or salmon.
Where to get it: Joy Indian Restaurant – Healthy, Seafood, Indian, Vegetarian, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Simple Grilled Chicken Salad

Grilled chicken, avocado, tomatoes, onion, cucumber and carrots over mesclun greens.
Where to get it: Salad Creations – Healthy, Salads, Soup, Dinner

Grilled Fish

Choose tilapia or wild salmon. Served with brown rice and choice of either sauteed or blanched dark greens, or garden veggies. Choose a side — Sauteed Dark Greens
Where to get it: The Organic Grill – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Grill, Breakfast, Organic, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Frittata

A tempting mix of organic eggs with potatoes, green peppers, soy sausage and onions, baked in a cast iron frying pan. Served with a cashew drizzle, fresh tomato and sprouts.
Where to get it: The Organic Grill – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Grill, Breakfast, Organic, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Greens Salad and Salmon Roll

Nori seaweed sheet rolled up with brown rice, steamed salmon, kale, broccoli, served with refreshingly cool green salad and a carrot ginger dressing.
Where to get it: Village Natural – Asian, Sandwiches, Healthy, Salads, Vegetarian, Dinner

Gluten-Free Balsamic Salmon Salad

Grilled salmon filet marinated in balsamic vinaigrette with tomatoes, cucumbers and sprouts over mixed greens. Choose a style — Dressing on the Side.
Where to get it: Nature’s Grill (Court St) – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Wraps, Fresh Fruits, Dinner

Grilled Wild Salmon Burger

Juicy, tender wild Pacific salmon patty topped with grilled onions and peppers.
Where to get it: Village Natural – Asian, Sandwiches, Healthy, Salads, Vegetarian, Dinner
