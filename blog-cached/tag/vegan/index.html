It’s hard to find decent vegan take out options in NYC. That’s where Bask comes in. After learning about your health goals, taste preferences and any special desires or needs, Bask’s intelligent food filtering algorithm gets to work figuring out which menu items are the best match for what you’re craving, day or night.

Ozu Tofu Burger

Tofu, hijiki, burdock, shiitake, edamame, garlic, ginger, carrots, onions, and tahini. Topped with tomato sauce, served with a side of steamed greens.
Where to get it: Ozu Restaurant – Asian, Healthy, Salads, Japanese, Sushi, Vegetarian, Dessert, Soup, Low Fat, Kosher-Style, Dinner

Raw Kale Salad

Raw kale, collard greens, carrots, scallions, tomatoes, hemp seeds, and olives with a sweet herb dressing.
Where to get it: The Organic Grill – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Grill, Breakfast, Organic, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Quinoa Salad

With lime, scallion and mint, candy beets, avocado, pumpkin seeds and toasted cumin.
Where to get it: M.O.B. – American, Healthy, Salads, Vegetarian, Low Fat, Organic, Low Carb, Vegan, Gluten-Free, New American, Dinner

Ozu Roll

Kabocha, beans, tahini, eggplant and tofu wrapped in a whole wheat tortilla. Served with either salad or steamed root vegetables.
Choose a side — Root Vegetables
Where to get it: Ozu Restaurant – Asian, Healthy, Salads, Japanese, Sushi, Vegetarian, Dessert, Soup, Low Fat, Kosher-Style, Dinner

Organic Oatmeal

With your choice of toppings.
Would you like toppings? — Walnuts
Where to get it: Blue Dog Cafe (56th St) – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Dinner

Green Salad

Avocado, sunflower sprouts, cucumber, broccoli, peas, celery and apple over kale, romaine and radicchio mix.
Choose a dressing — Regular Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
Where to get it: Salad Creations – Healthy, Salads, Soup, Dinner

Dal Makhani

Simmered black lentils and red kidney beans finished with tomatoes, ginger, cumin, onions, and fresh garlic.
Where to get it: Bombay Palace – Healthy, Indian, Vegetarian, Dinner

Healthy Vegan Salad

Steamed broccoli, carrots, avocado, sunflower seeds, black beans and raisin
Where to get it: Healthy Oasis – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Vegetarian, Soup, Eclectic, Wraps, Hamburgers, Dinner

Warm Wild Mushroom Salad

Sauteed assorted daily fresh wild mushroom with olive oil and truffle puree, served on a bed of spinach.
Where to get it: Aji 53 – Asian, Japanese, Sushi, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Portobello Mushroom Burrito

Portobello burger, soy cheddar, black beans, organic brown rice, roasted onions, greens and salsa. Made with a whole wheat wrap.
Where to get it: Healthy Oasis – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Vegetarian, Soup, Eclectic, Wraps, Hamburgers, Dinner
