Looking for the best vegetarian take out ideas in your area? Bask is the easy new way to order healthy, delicious food to your doorstep. After learning about your health goals, taste preferences and any special desires or needs, Bask’s intelligent food filtering algorithm gets to work figuring out which menu items are the best match for what you’re craving, day or night.

Crunchy Cabbage Salad with Orange Tahini Dressing

Cabbage, green onion, carrot, cilantro, cauliflower, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, all tossed in a orange tahini dressing.
Where to get it: The Organic Grill – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Grill, Breakfast, Organic, Lunch Specials, Dinner

2 Organic Cage Free Brown Eggs

Served with your choice of side and toast. Would you like extras, get fresh fruit
Where to get it: Sanford’s Restaurant

Quinoa Salad

Vegan. Wheat and gluten free. Quinoa, avocado, red onion, cherry tomato, basil cayenne vinaigrette (no lettuce).
Where to get it: Bogota Latin Bistro - Sandwiches, Healthy, Salads, Chicken, Vegetarian, Lunch Specials, Latin American, Colombian, Gyro, Dinner

Vegetarian Pure Salad

Broccoli, carrots, tomatoes, edamame, avocado, bell pepper, corn, alfalfa sprouts and sunflower seeds over baby spinach and romaine lettuce. Recommended with balsamic vinaigrette. Choose a salad dressing — Balsamic Vinaigrette
Where to get it: Pure and Fresh – Sandwiches, Pasta, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Smoothies and Juices, Lunch, Dinner

Sweetie Salad

Toasted almonds, apples, beets, strawberries, feta, orange and cucumber over kale, romaine, radicchio mix. Choose a dressing — Regular Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
Where to get it: Salad Creations – Healthy, Salads, Soup, Dinner

Healthy Tofu Salad

Grilled tofu, fresh greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, eggplant and sunflower seeds. Choose a dressing — Balsamic Vinaigrette
Where to get it: Healthy Oasis – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Vegetarian, Soup, Eclectic, Wraps, Hamburgers, Dinner

Gluten Free Chopped Salad

Arugula, romaine, tomato, basil, onion, roasted red pepper, black olives and cannellini beans.
Where to get it: Sambuca – Italian, Pasta, Gluten-Free, Dinner

Veggie Omelet

Peppers, onions, broccoli, cauliflower and tomatoes.
Where to get it: Carrizal Bar and GrillBreakfast, Latin American, Lunch, Dinner

Roasted Cauliflower and French Lentil Salad

Capers, raisins and sherry vinaigrette.
Where to get it: Camaje Bistro – Sandwiches, Breakfast, Dessert, French, Organic, Dinner

Miso Soup

Our organic soybean paste in vegetable broth with tofu, veggies and wakame seaweed.
Where to get it: Village Natural – Asian, Sandwiches, Healthy, Salads, Vegetarian, Dinner
