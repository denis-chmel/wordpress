Looking for the best paleo delivery ideas in your area? Bask is the easy new way to order healthy, delicious food to your doorstep. After learning about your health goals, taste preferences and any special desires or needs, Bask’s intelligent food filtering algorithm gets to work figuring out which menu items are the best match for what you’re craving, day or night.

Seafood Grill Entree

Combination of grilled octopus, calamari, shrimps and salmon topped with extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice.
Where to get it: Aliada Restaurant

Grilled Chicken Salad

Mixed greens, grilled chicken, eggs, cucumber, tomatoes and vinaigrette dressing.
Where to get it: Dulce Vida Cafe

Tandoori Halibut

Fillet of halibut marinated with mint, ginger, garlic, green pepper, and cilantro. Cooked in a pit oven made with clay and heated by burning charcoal.
Where to get it: Indus Valley – Healthy, Salads, Grill, Chicken, Seafood, Indian, Vegetarian, Dessert, Soup, Coffee and Tea, Dinner

Shrimp w/ Ginger

Sauteed shrimp with fresh ginger, onions, chili, mushroom, green onion, pineapple.
Where to get it: Teeda Thai Cuisine – Asian, Healthy, Thai, Noodles, Lunch Specials, Dinner

Energy Thai Chicken Plate

Chicken breast, onions and tomatoes baked with tahini and served with eggs.
Where to get it: Energy Fuel – Sandwiches, American, Healthy, Salads, Breakfast, Wraps, Smoothies and Juices, Hamburgers, Dinner

Steamed Red Snapper

Filet fish with lime juice and ginger. Served on the bed of seasonal vegetable.
Where to get it: Nahm Thai Kitchen

The Hanger

Marinated grass-fed hanger steak char-grilled to order. Choose two sides — Field Greens with Balsamic Vinaigrette
Where to get it: Savory – Sandwiches, American, Pasta, Hamburgers, Dinner

Grilled Calamari Salad

Fresh grilled calamari tossed on greens with tomatoes.
Where to get it: Antalia

Pan Seared Wild Salmon

Wild Atlantic salmon topped with zesty lemon dill sauce. Served with your choice of side.
Where to get it: Food Craze -Pizza, American, Pasta, Grill, Hamburgers, Dinner

Chicken Tikka Kebab

Cubes of chicken breast marinated in ginger and herbs, slowly cooked on skewers in the tandoor. Clay oven cooked.
Where to get it: Desi Grill Indian Restaurant – Healthy, Seafood, Indian, Vegetarian, Dessert, Lunch Specials, Dinner
